Location: Iowa City, IA
Business Description: Jarrett Mitchell created Cobra Verde while selling coffee out White
Rabbit, a local art and apparel store, followed by Wake Up Iowa, a
coffee shop that closed in 2013. Even after the coffee shop closed,
customer enthusiasm for Cobra Verde encouraged Mitchell to
continue producing the cold, coffee-infused energy drink. Mitchell
continues roasting his coffee beans locally and aspires to take
Cobra Verde national, a goal that is currently set at 160
independent co-ops and farmers markets across the country.
Product Description: Classified as an energy drink, Cobra Verde is a green-coffee infused
refrigerated beverage served out of the bottle, on ice, or mixed
with a variety of spirits. Though made from unroasted coffee beans,
the flavor is overwhelmingly citrusy and crisp. Cobra Verde’s
proprietary recipe consists of green coffee, ginger, lime, lemon, and
cane sugar. Comparable energy drinks typically contain at least two
or three times the amount of ingredients. Now available in 10 oz
glass bottles, Mitchell plans to offer 32 oz growlers in coming months.
The product is publicized primarily via word of mouth, at farmers
markets, and a passive social media campaign. Fans are
encouraged to post Cobra Verde recipes on the company’s
Facebook page, such as margaritas and the Snake Charmer, a
blend of Cobra Verde and Champaign.
